Featured Article

The Future of Tech in Education

By Dr. Anita Rao, Professor of Computer Science

Education is evolving with artificial intelligence, virtual labs, and personalized learning platforms. Colleges that adopt these tools early are preparing students for careers that do not yet exist. This article explores how our institution is integrating smart classrooms and digital resources to enhance learning outcomes.

Student feedback has been overwhelmingly positive. Workshops on coding, data science, and cybersecurity have seen record enrollment. We believe that combining traditional pedagogy with modern technology creates the best environment for the next generation of engineers and innovators.

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” — Nelson Mandela
