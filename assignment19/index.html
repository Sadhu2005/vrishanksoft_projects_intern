About the Event

TechFest is our college's flagship technical festival, bringing together students from across the country for hackathons, workshops, and competitions. This year we focus on AI, cybersecurity, and sustainable tech.

Participants get hands-on experience with industry tools, mentorship from experts, and a chance to showcase projects. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced coder, there is something for everyone.

Register early to secure your spot and get access to exclusive pre-event workshops and resource kits.

Registration Guidelines

Please read before registering:

1. All participants must be currently enrolled in a recognized college or university. A valid ID card is required at the venue.

2. Team size for hackathon is 2–4 members. Solo participation is allowed for coding contests and workshops.

3. Registration is free. Slots are limited and will be allotted on a first-come, first-served basis. Once you submit the form, you will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours.

4. Participants must bring their own laptops for hackathon and workshop tracks. Power and Wi‑Fi will be provided at the venue.

5. By registering, you agree to abide by the code of conduct and the rules published on the event website. Any violation may lead to disqualification.

6. For any queries, write to techfest@college.edu or call the helpline 1800-XXX-XXXX between 9 AM and 6 PM.
