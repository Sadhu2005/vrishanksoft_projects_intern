Multimedia Format Support

We highlight formats that run smoothly on modern browsers.

Video Formats (MP4, WebM, Ogg)
  • MP4 (H.264) ? Widely supported with hardware acceleration.
  • WebM (VP8/VP9) ? Great for streaming and compression efficiency.
  • Ogg (Theora) ? Open format for browsers that prefer royalty-free codecs.
Audio Formats (MP3, WAV, Ogg)
  • MP3 ? Great compatibility and relatively small file size.
  • WAV ? Higher fidelity for studio-ready content.
  • Ogg (Vorbis) ? Open-source option with excellent quality.
